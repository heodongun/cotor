# Design Directions (for product interfaces)

## 1) Precision & Density
- Best for: admin, devtools, operations panels
- Traits: tight spacing, restrained color, high information density
- Defaults: 8px grid, subtle radius (6-8), borders over shadows

## 2) Warmth & Approachability
- Best for: collaboration, SMB tools, onboarding-heavy products
- Traits: generous spacing, softer corners, friendly semantics
- Defaults: 8px grid with larger vertical rhythm, soft elevation

## 3) Sophistication & Trust
- Best for: fintech, enterprise, compliance-sensitive UX
- Traits: calm contrast, deliberate hierarchy, conservative motion
- Defaults: cool neutral palette, strict typography, moderate density

## 4) Boldness & Clarity
- Best for: modern data products, executive dashboards
- Traits: clear contrast, strong sectioning, assertive CTA
- Defaults: explicit color hierarchy, stronger scale steps

## 5) Utility & Function
- Best for: GitHub-like functional UIs
- Traits: minimal decoration, robust defaults, fast scanability
- Defaults: neutral-first tokens, minimal motion, practical spacing

## 6) Data & Analysis
- Best for: analytics/BI
- Traits: table/chart readability, alignment rigor, data-first hierarchy
- Defaults: monospaced numerics, chart-safe palette, dense tables

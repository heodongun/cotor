# Vercel/Next.js/Turbo/v0 Brand Notes (Quick)

Derived from Geist brands guidance:

- Do not alter trademarks/logos or create confusingly similar marks.
- Use symbol-only variants only when full logo cannot fit or in multi-brand icon contexts.
- Keep adequate clear space around logos.
- Do not imply sponsorship/endorsement or affiliation.
- Do not use marks in a misleading or defamatory context.
- Keep attribution and legal review in mind for public/commercial collateral.

## Practical UI guidance

- Partner rows: normalize visual size by cap height, not box width.
- Keep monochrome treatment only if official brand kit allows it.
- Do not animate logos aggressively.
- Keep clickable logos clearly linked to relevant destination and label accessibly.
